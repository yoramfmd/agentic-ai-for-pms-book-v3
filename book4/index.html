Why Agentic AI Products Fail

Building Channel 1 and the Supervisory Layer That Governs It

By Yoram Friedman

Every agentic product is two products: the agent that acts, and the supervisory layer that governs it. Most teams build the first and assume the second takes care of itself. It does not. This book is organized around the supervisory layer, its four dimensions (technical, organizational, regulatory, and moral), its failure modes, and the discipline required to build it deliberately.

Twenty-two chapters across six parts, from deciding whether to build at all to carrying accountability for the people your agent will never see.
